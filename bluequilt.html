There is no better word than we.

2024

Quilt, Denim Blend, Cotton

There is no better word than we draws text from Ibram X. Kendi and Keisha N. Blain's Black history anthology, Four Hundred Souls. The quilt is a nod to the techniques of Gee's Bend quilters and references obscured parts of Black history.
